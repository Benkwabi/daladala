Meet Our Founder

The vision behind TANZANIA THE GREAT HOUSE

Mr. Severine Tinda

Founder & CEO of Dalali Wa Mkoa Real Estate Company

A pioneer in Tanzania's real estate sector, Mr. Tinda established Dalali Wa Mkoa Real Estate Company in 2008 with a revolutionary vision to transform the country's property landscape. His concept of "Tanzania The Great House" embodies the belief that every Tanzanian deserves quality housing and every investor should find profitable opportunities in our growing market.

"We don't just sell properties - we create homes, build communities, and shape Tanzania's future. This is why we call it Tanzania The Great House - because from the humblest studio to the grandest villa, every space has its place in our nation's story."

His Journey

Starting as a small-scale property agent in Dar es Salaam, Mr. Tinda's deep understanding of local communities and commitment to ethical practices propelled Dalali Wa Mkoa to become one of Tanzania's most trusted real estate brands. His hands-on approach ensures every transaction meets his high standards of integrity and customer satisfaction.
